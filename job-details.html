Senior Fashion Designer

Tab on Fashion - New York, NY

Full-time Senior Level $80k - $120k Posted 2 days ago

Job Description

We are seeking a talented Senior Fashion Designer to join our creative team at Tab on Fashion. The ideal candidate will have a strong portfolio demonstrating innovative design concepts and a deep understanding of current fashion trends.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead the design process from concept to final product
  • Create seasonal collections that align with brand aesthetics
  • Collaborate with product development and manufacturing teams
  • Research fashion trends and consumer preferences
  • Mentor junior designers and provide creative direction

Requirements:

  • 7+ years of experience in fashion design
  • Bachelor's degree in Fashion Design or related field
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and CAD software
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating creative abilities
  • Excellent communication and leadership skills

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and bonus structure
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) with company match
  • Generous PTO and holidays
  • Professional development opportunities
